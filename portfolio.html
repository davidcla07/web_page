This project was developed to the foundation "Échele Cabeza" with aiming to measure the impact of this project through data science in Colombian society.

We develop an app in which the users can view valuable information, regarding the consumption of drugs. Further, we developed a model to simulate the analysis by Echele Cabeza to find out if the user bought the substance that told him.

Prediction Soccer Results Project

Through this project, I did a model to predict the results of soccer matches and compare them with the predictions of the bookmakers most important in the market betting.

To predict the results I used logistic models and different techniques like feature engineering to find out a good model. Comparing the results with the predictions of the bookmakers the model got similar results with a good performance.

Population affected by floodplains in the north of Colombia

In this project, I used ArcGIS to analyze the flood area of rivers and streams in the north of Colombia, and find out the population affected. I used the processing of satellite images for this project.
